Product Description

This classic button-down shirt combines timeless style with innovative adaptive features. Hidden magnetic closures replace traditional buttons, making dressing significantly easier for individuals with limited dexterity, arthritis, or one-handed functionality.

The shirt maintains the polished appearance of a traditional button-down while offering the convenience of magnetic technology. Perfect for professional settings, casual wear, or any occasion where you want to look sharp without the struggle of traditional buttons.

Key Features

  • Hidden Magnetic Closures: Strong magnets disguised as traditional buttons align and close automatically
  • One-Handed Dressing: Can be easily put on and taken off with minimal dexterity
  • Professional Appearance: Looks like a standard button-down shirt from the outside
  • Wrinkle-Resistant Fabric: Easy-care material that stays looking fresh
  • Extended Tail: Longer back hem stays tucked in when seated
  • Soft Collar: No-irritation collar design for all-day comfort

Materials & Care

Materials: 65% Cotton, 35% Polyester blend for durability and comfort

Care Instructions: Machine wash cold, tumble dry low. Magnets are rust-resistant and designed to withstand regular washing. Do not iron directly over magnetic closures.
