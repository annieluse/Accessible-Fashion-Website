Our Mission

Our mission with this research project is to provide resources and opportunities to those who seek adaptive clothes. Finding reliable information on available brands and leading research can be difficult at times. The main goal of this website is to compile all of the information gathered throughout the terms and condense the information into one palatable source.

About Us

We are a team of Oregon State University students in the Design for Social Impact program (DSI). This team aims to understand barriers in mainstream fashion and co-create solutions with disabled community members. Our work focuses on inclusive design principles, clear product information, and accessibility-first navigation.

Learn more about the DSI program here!

Project Sponsor

Bolanle Dahunsi is an Assistant Professor in the College of Business at Oregon State University. She teaches courses in Digital Design for Apparel and Apparel Design with Draping. She hopes to help her students learn to make informed design decisions that balance aesthetics, functionality, accessibility, and sustainability.
