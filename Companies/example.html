About the Company

Adaptive Brand Co. was founded in 2015 with a mission to make fashion accessible to everyone. The company specializes in creating stylish, functional clothing that incorporates adaptive features without compromising on aesthetics.

Working closely with occupational therapists, disability advocates, and people with lived experience, Adaptive Brand Co. has developed innovative solutions that address real-world challenges faced by individuals with diverse mobility needs.

Accessibility Features

  • Magnetic Closures: Easy-to-use magnetic buttons and zippers that replace traditional fasteners
  • Seated Fit: Specially designed cuts for wheelchair users with longer backs and shorter fronts
  • Side & Back Openings: Strategic placement for easier dressing and undressing
  • Sensory-Friendly Materials: Soft, tagless fabrics that minimize irritation
  • Adjustable Features: Velcro and elastic elements for customizable fit
  • One-Handed Dressing: Designs that can be put on with limited hand mobility

Company Values

Inclusive Design: Every product is designed with input from the disability community to ensure it meets real needs.

Quality Materials: Using sustainable, high-quality fabrics that are both durable and comfortable for extended wear.

Affordable Accessibility: Committed to keeping adaptive clothing affordable and accessible to all income levels.
